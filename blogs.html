Khush Rehne ka Science – Chhote Chhote Ways se Apka Mood Behtar Banaye Rakhein

Posted on: 11 August 2025

Khushi sirf ek emotion nahi, balki ek science hai. Jab hum chhote-chhote positive habits apnate hain, to hamare dimaag me dopamine aur serotonin jaise “happy hormones” release hote hain, jo mood ko uplift karte hain.

Yeh Rahe Kuch Simple Ways:

  • Subah uthte hi deep breathing & gratitude practice karein.
  • Din me kam se kam 10 minute dhup me baithein.
  • Kisi apne ko call karke unka haal puchhein.
  • Din me 5 minute bina phone ke sirf apne saath bitayein.
  • Chhoti chhoti jeet ka celebration karein.

Jab aap ye chhote steps adopt karte ho, to aap apne mood ko naturally positive rakh pate ho. Khushi ek daily practice hai – aaj se shuru kijiye!
